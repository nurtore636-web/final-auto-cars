Audi A6 C4 — Full History & Specifications

Production: 1994–1997 | Factory: Ingolstadt, Germany

The Audi A6 C4 succeeded the Audi 100 and represented a major leap forward for the brand. It featured improved aerodynamics, enhanced safety, and a variety of modern engine options.

  • Engines: From inline-4 to V6 petrol and diesel versions
  • Drivetrain: Front-wheel or Quattro AWD
  • Fun fact: Introduced cutting-edge technology like ABS and advanced comfort systems.

More: Audi A6 — Wikipedia

BMW E34 — Full History & Specifications

Production: 1987–1996

The BMW E34 is part of the fifth generation of the 5 Series, celebrated for its balance, performance, and reliability. It defined the “golden era” of BMW engineering.

  • Engines: Inline-4 and Inline-6 petrol & diesel options
  • Highlights: Precision handling and advanced safety systems
  • Fun fact: Many E34 models are still beloved by collectors for their build quality.

More: BMW E34 — Wikipedia

Mercedes-Benz W210 — Full History & Specifications

Production: 1995–2002

The W210 introduced a bold twin-headlight design and advanced safety features, marking a new era for the E-Class. It remains one of the most durable and iconic sedans ever built.

  • Engines: Wide range from inline-4 to V8
  • Strengths: Comfort, longevity, and innovation
  • Fun fact: The AMG version became a symbol of performance luxury.
